ABOUT PHISHING


Attackers frequently use phishing because it is simpler to fool a victim into clicking a malicious link that seems authentic than to try to get past a computer's security measures. The malicious links inside the message's body are made to look as though they lead to the faked company by utilising its logos and other official materials.Any unregistered domain name is open to registration by the attacker. This URL component can only be changed once. The phisher may always generate a new URL by changing FreeURL. Due to the distinctive element of the website domain, security defences have trouble identifying phishing domains. It is simple to stop a person from accessing a domain that has been identified as fraudulent.
